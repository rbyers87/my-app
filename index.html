Are You Ready to Serve?

Joining our department requires dedication and meeting specific criteria. Review these minimum requirements:

  • Age: Must be at least 21 years old.
  • Education: High School Diploma or GED required; college degree preferred.
  • Physical Fitness: Must pass a rigorous physical agility test.
  • Character: Must maintain a clean criminal record and pass comprehensive background checks.

The Recruitment Process

Our process is designed to assess not only your skills, but your character and commitment. Follow these general steps:

  1. Initial Application: Complete our online application form.
  2. Written & Physical Testing: Participate in aptitude tests and the physical fitness assessment.
  3. Interviews & Background Check: Undergo multiple rounds of interviews with department leadership.
  4. Academy Training: Successfully complete the rigorous Police Academy program.
